Classical Qur'anic Arabic (CQA)

This is a weeklong course taught by the renowned teacher of Arabic and the Director, Arabic Department of IIDR, Dr Muhammad Mustafa.

This course will be divided into two groups, Basic and Advanced. Basic level will assume a basic grasp of Arabic such as reading and writing alphabets. Module: 4 hours lecture, 1 hour Lab.

Advanced level prerequisites will consist of a good knowledge of Arabic grammar with skills of reading, speaking and writing basic Arabic. Module: 4 hours lecture, 1 hour lab

Course Dates: 1st July 2001 - 7th July 2001.
